Blockchain Asset Management (BAM)

This is a decentralized web application based on the blockchain technology. This showcases the usability of the modern blockchain technology within a classic supply chain context. The digital assets, you are able to create are the digital hash of physical ones within the supply chain. With this web application, you are able to track the way of the assets through the chain, verify the ownership, and have access to relevant information like timestamps of every transaction ( which could be a step within the supply chain).
